International

NEBOSH IGC

National Examination Board in Occupational Safety and Health - International General Certificate

Duration 3 Months
Language English
Eligibility 12th Pass / Graduate
Mode Classroom / Online

About NEBOSH IGC

NEBOSH IGC is one of the most respected and widely recognized health and safety qualifications globally. It provides a broad understanding of health and safety principles and is ideal for managers, supervisors, and anyone with health and safety responsibilities.

What You'll Learn

  • Health and safety management systems
  • Risk assessment and control measures
  • Workplace hazard identification
  • Fire safety and emergency procedures
  • Chemical and biological hazards
  • Physical and psychological health factors

Career Opportunities

HSE Officer, Safety Manager, Safety Consultant, EHS Engineer, Risk Assessment Specialist

International

IOSH Managing Safely

Institution of Occupational Safety and Health - Managing Safely Certificate

Duration 4-5 Days
Language English
Eligibility Any Graduate
Mode Classroom / Online

About IOSH Managing Safely

IOSH Managing Safely is designed for managers and supervisors in any sector. It provides the knowledge and tools required to handle health and safety responsibilities effectively. This certification is perfect for those who need to understand their safety duties.

What You'll Learn

  • Introducing managing safely
  • Assessing and controlling risks
  • Understanding responsibilities
  • Identifying common hazards
  • Investigating incidents
  • Measuring performance

Career Opportunities

Team Leaders, Supervisors, Managers, Project Managers, Department Heads

Language English / Hindi
Eligibility 10th Pass
Mode Classroom + Practical

About Fire Safety Certification

Our Fire Safety Certification program provides comprehensive training in fire prevention, detection, and emergency response. This hands-on course includes live fire drills and practical training with firefighting equipment.

What You'll Learn

  • Fire science and behavior
  • Fire prevention techniques
  • Fire detection and alarm systems
  • Firefighting equipment operation
  • Emergency evacuation procedures
  • First aid and rescue operations

First Aid & CPR Certification

Emergency Medical Response and Life-Saving Techniques

Duration 2-3 Days
Language English / Hindi
Eligibility Anyone 18+
Mode Practical Training

About First Aid Certification

Learn essential life-saving skills with our First Aid & CPR certification program. This hands-on course teaches you how to respond to medical emergencies, perform CPR, and provide immediate care before professional help arrives.

What You'll Learn

  • CPR and AED usage
  • Wound care and bleeding control
  • Fracture and sprain management
  • Burns and electrical injuries
  • Choking and respiratory emergencies
  • Medical emergency response

Career Opportunities

First Aid Trainer, Safety Officer, Emergency Responder, Workplace First Aider
