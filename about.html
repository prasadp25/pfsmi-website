Who We Are

About PFSMI

PROTECTHER Fire & Safety Management Institute (PFSMI) is India's premier institution dedicated to professional fire and safety education.

We are committed to developing skilled safety professionals who can make a real difference in protecting lives and property. Our comprehensive programs combine theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on training, preparing students for successful careers in the fire and safety industry.

With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we provide an environment where students can learn, grow, and excel in their chosen field of safety management.

Our Vision

"Greater Good of Society"

To be the leading institution in fire and safety education, creating a safer world through excellence in training, research, and innovation. We envision a society where every individual is equipped with the knowledge and skills to prevent accidents and respond effectively to emergencies.

Our Mission

"Pragmatic Solutions"

To provide world-class fire and safety education that combines academic rigor with practical expertise. We are committed to producing industry-ready professionals who can implement pragmatic solutions to safety challenges across all sectors.
