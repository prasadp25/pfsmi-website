FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

What are the admission requirements?

Requirements vary by course. Generally, candidates need a minimum of 10th/12th pass for diploma courses and graduation for PG diploma courses. Contact our admissions office for specific requirements.

Do you offer part-time courses?

Yes, all our courses are available in both full-time and part-time modes. Part-time classes are typically held on weekends or evenings to accommodate working professionals.

Is placement assistance provided?

Yes, we provide 100% placement assistance to all our students. Our placement cell works closely with leading companies like Tata Projects, L&T, Adani, and many more.

Are the certifications government recognized?

Yes, our courses are affiliated with MSBTE (Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education) and other recognized bodies. Our certifications are valid across India and internationally.

What is the fee structure?

Fees vary by course and duration. We offer competitive pricing with flexible EMI options available. Contact our admissions team for detailed fee information.

Do you provide hostel facilities?

We can assist students in finding suitable accommodation near our campus. Contact our student services team for accommodation assistance.
