Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

Check if you meet the requirements for your desired course

Diploma Courses

  • 10th Pass (SSC) from recognized board
  • Minimum 35% aggregate marks
  • Age: 17 years and above
  • Basic English communication skills
Applicable for:

Diploma in Fire & Safety, Diploma in Industrial Safety Management

Most Popular

PG Diploma Courses

  • Graduate in any discipline
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Age: No upper limit
  • Working professionals welcome
Applicable for:

PG Diploma in Industrial Safety, PG Diploma in Fire & Safety Management

Certification Courses

  • 12th Pass (HSC) or equivalent
  • No minimum marks required
  • Age: 18 years and above
  • Industry experience preferred
Applicable for:

NEBOSH IGC, IOSH Managing Safely, Fire Safety Certifications
